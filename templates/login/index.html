Frequently Asked Questions

How do you know an email is verified?

We assign a quality score to every email we search for. Verified emails are directly validated with the server and maintain at least a 98% deliverability rate.

What about the emails that aren’t verified?

Emails with lower than a 98% quality score are provided to you for free. We use a color gradient scheme to indicate our level of certainty for each unverified email.

Do you offer free trials?

If you register an account here you can evaluate our service and receive 20 verified emails for free.

How do rollovers work?

If you don’t use all of your email credits in a given month, the credit will be added to your credit for the next month. You will be able to use any of your credits until you cancel.

What if I need to cancel?

You may cancel your subscription at any time during the billing period. After you cancel, you will be able to continue using your remaining credits until the end of your billing period.

What if I need more emails validated than the plans you offer?

Please contact our sales team at sales@pursuit.ai.
